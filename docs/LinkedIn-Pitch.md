---

What if every maintenance request a tenant submits got a useful response in
under five seconds — and the right vendor was on the way in under five minutes?

That's FixRoute.

Property managers today spend over 40% of their day reading maintenance
requests, triaging urgency, and making phone calls to dispatch vendors. It's
manual, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

FixRoute is an AI-powered maintenance operations platform that automates the
entire intake-to-dispatch flow. Tenants submit a request with text, photos, or
voice. Our AI classifies the category and urgency, provides self-serve
troubleshooting for simple issues, and dispatches the right vendor with
calibrated priority — all in under five minutes.

The result: property managers reclaim their day, tenants get immediate
acknowledgment, and emergencies stop waiting behind dripping faucets.

Under the hood, FixRoute runs on a multi-tenant cloud architecture with
PostgreSQL plus pgvector for vector embeddings, Azure Kubernetes Service for
multi-region disaster recovery with a four-hour RTO and fifteen-minute RPO,
and Microsoft Entra ID for enterprise-grade authentication. We built it with
Domain-Driven Design, test-driven development, and a strong constitution of
principles — every AI input and output passes through safety guardrails, every
record is audit-logged, and tenants can request erasure of their personal data
with a thirty-day guarantee.

We're already delivering a deployable vertical slice covering intake, AI
triage, vendor matching, dispatch, cost estimates, and spend analytics. The
next phase: native mobile apps, computer-vision damage assessment, and
predictive maintenance using historical work-order embeddings.

If you're a property management company losing hours every day to manual
triage, or a tenant who's ever waited a week for a fix — FixRoute is for you.

## 📌 Posting tips

- **Hook in the first line.** The first sentence ("What if every maintenance
  request…") works as the LinkedIn preview text.
- **Pause after questions.** The two rhetorical questions are natural
  engagement moments — pause 1-2 seconds after each.
- **Add a personal story.** Open with a specific moment ("Last month I watched
  a property manager triage 47 requests in one morning…") to humanize the pitch.
- **End with a clear CTA.** "Comment 'FIX' if you'd like a demo" or "DM me for
  the technical deep-dive."
- **Best posting times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM in your audience's timezone.
